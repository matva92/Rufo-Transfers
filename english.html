About

Private transfers with personalized service in Buenos Aires.

Rufo Transfers provides door-to-door rides for passengers looking for comfort, punctuality and direct communication with the driver.

Every booking is handled in advance with route and schedule confirmation, so your trip is clear from the first message.

History

A company built one trip at a time.

2018

Operations started with a focus on private rides and human service.

2020

Consolidation in airport routes, business transfers and medium-distance services.

Today

Bilingual support, online quote and service details tailored to each passenger.
